About UNICYCLE

A student-first marketplace built to make campus life more affordable and sustainable.

Our Mission

UNICYCLE was created by students, for students. We believe that quality education shouldn't break the bank — and that means making it easier to find affordable second-hand items right within your campus community. From textbooks to tech, we help students save money while reducing waste.
